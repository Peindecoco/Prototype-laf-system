Main Instruction Page

How the Lost and Found Website Works

Use this page as your step-by-step guide for finding, claiming, or reporting an item. The gallery is always the first place to check, while the report page is the last resort when your item is not yet listed.

Open Gallery Report Lost Item
01

Start with the Home Gallery

The Home / Gallery page is the main page of the website. This is where users can browse found items, review item locations, and begin the claiming process.

  • Open the gallery and look through the listed items.
  • Use the category filter to narrow down the results faster.
  • Check the item photo, name, and location before claiming.
02

How Claiming Works

When you click Claim Item, the system asks for your details and your description of the item. The website then uses ChatGPT-based matching to compare your answers with the stored found-item details.

  • Provide your name and contact information.
  • Describe the item honestly, including identifying or secret details.
  • Add the color, size, and shape as accurately as possible.
03

AI Matching and Claim Result

The AI computes a match score to determine whether your claim is strong enough. If your details match the stored item information, the system will confirm that your claim is successful.

  • A successful match means you may proceed with claiming the item.
  • If the match is not strong enough, the system will tell you that the claim was unsuccessful.
  • More accurate details improve your chance of a correct match.
04

After a Successful Claim

If the website confirms your claim, take a screenshot of the successful claim message. This screenshot serves as your proof when you proceed to collect the item face to face.

  1. Screenshot the successful claim confirmation.
  2. Keep your screenshot ready for verification.
  3. Proceed to the designated face-to-face claiming or collection process.

Last Resort Process

When to Use the Report Page

The Report page should be used only if you cannot find your lost item in the main gallery. This allows the admin or FTS team to take notice of the missing item and search for it.

Report the lost item

Enter the item name, description, where it was lost, and other details that can help identify it.

Provide your email or contact details

Include your email on the report page so the admin or FTS can reach you with updates if the item is found.

Wait for the search process

The admin or FTS will monitor the report, watch for new found items, and try to locate the missing item.

Receive an update after two days if found

If the item is located after two days, the FTS team will email the user using the contact information provided in the report.

Best Practices for Users

  • Always check the gallery first before submitting a report.
  • Be truthful and specific when describing your item.
  • Prepare identifying details that only the real owner would know.
  • Save any successful confirmation message or screenshot.

Quick Summary

Find item in gallery? Claim it there first.

Claim approved? Screenshot the success message and collect the item face to face.

Item not in gallery? Go to the report page and submit your lost-item report for admin / FTS review.
